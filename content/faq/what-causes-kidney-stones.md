---
title: "What causes kidney stones and how can I prevent them?"
category: "Conditions"
---

Kidney stones develop when urine becomes too concentrated with minerals like calcium, oxalate, and uric acid. In Nepal, dehydration during hot summer months is a leading cause, along with excessive dietary salt, high animal protein intake, and family history. You can prevent them by drinking 3 to 4 liters of water daily, reducing salt and red meat consumption, and adding citrus juices like lemon to your water.
