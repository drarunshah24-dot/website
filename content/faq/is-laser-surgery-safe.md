---
title: "Is minimally invasive laser surgery safe for elderly patients or those on blood thinners?"
category: "Treatments"
---

Yes, advanced laser procedures such as HoLEP for prostate enlargement and RIRS for kidney stones are exceptionally safe. Because high-precision lasers (like Holmium and Thulium) seal blood vessels instantly as they vaporize tissue, there is minimal to zero blood loss. This makes laser surgery the gold standard even for elderly patients, cardiac patients, or those taking blood-thinning medications.
