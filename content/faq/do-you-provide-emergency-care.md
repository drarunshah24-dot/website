Yes, National Urology Center provides round-the-clock 24/7 emergency urological support. Urological emergencies such as acute kidney stone pain (renal colic), complete inability to pass urine (acute urinary retention), severe blood in urine (hematuria), or scrotal trauma require immediate specialist evaluation.
