---
title: "What are the early warning signs of prostate enlargement (BPH)?"
category: "Conditions"
---

Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) causes progressive urinary symptoms as the enlarged gland compresses the urethra. Watch for: frequent urination (especially waking up 2+ times at night), a weak or interrupted urine stream, difficulty starting urination, a sudden urgent need to urinate, and the feeling that your bladder is never completely empty.
