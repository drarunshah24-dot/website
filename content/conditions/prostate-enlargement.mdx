Prostate enlargement, also known as BPH (Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia), is a common condition in men over 50 where the prostate gland grows larger than normal and compresses the urethra.

## Symptoms
- Frequent urination, especially at night (nocturia)
- Difficulty starting urination or a weak stream
- Feeling of incomplete bladder emptying
- Urgency to urinate
