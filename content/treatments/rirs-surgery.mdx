Retrograde Intrarenal Surgery (RIRS) is an advanced laser procedure for treating kidney stones without making any incisions on the kidney.

## How it works
The instrument is passed through the urethra, into the bladder, and then up into the kidney. A laser is then used to crush the stones into dust, which are naturally flushed out with urine.

## Benefits
- No cuts or stitches
- Faster recovery time
- Minimal pain
- Discharge within 24 hours
