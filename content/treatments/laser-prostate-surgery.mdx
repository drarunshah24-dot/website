Holmium Laser Enucleation of the Prostate (HoLEP) and Transurethral Resection of the Prostate (TURP) are advanced surgical treatments for Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH).

## HoLEP Procedure
HoLEP uses a holmium laser to remove the obstructing prostate tissue through the urethra. It is considered the gold standard for large prostates.

## Benefits
- No external incisions
- Minimal blood loss
- Shorter hospital stay (1-2 days)
- Rapid return to normal activities
- Long-lasting results
